Work Experience

During my time in the engineering program, I have met a number of industry professionals through workplace field trips and guest speakers who have inspired me through hands-on experiences as well as a glimpse into daily lives. These experiences have revealed new career paths that I never knew existed and helped me get a better grasp on what I want to do in the future. Additionally, through question and answer sessions, they have also shaped my academic path and shown me the steps I need to take to reach my dream of being a software engineer.

One of the field trips that stood out to me was the trip to Patriots Jet Team Foundation where I was able to get a closer look at the logistics and engineering behind a successful airshow. When I saw my first airshow as a child, I had always thought that it would be a relatively easy task to accomplish. However, our guide, a current show pilot, revealed many interesting things that I didn’t know before about the composition of the plane. For example, there were the smoke system, the horizontal and vertical stabilizers, elevators, fuel tanks, and rudder, many had to be modified for supersonic speeds. Additionally, other factors such as pressure, temperature, and humidity would also have to be taken into account when performing an airshow, which was something I had never taken into consideration before.

In addition to field trips, I also got the opportunity to talk to many industry professionals, with one particularly interesting person being Jason from Uber. Jason worked on Uber’s self-driving cars program and went into detail about how machine learning and advancements in technology will guide the future. Everything in the world runs on data, and by processing that data, many incredible things can be accomplished. Jason explained how his machine learning classification model worked with its neural network and how it processes large quantities of data to classify images and entities through its sensors. From both a hardware and software standpoint, many teams of engineers worked together and collaborated to develop an efficient and safe car which will go on to save the lives of many people in the future by preventing car accidents.
